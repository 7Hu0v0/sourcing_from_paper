## Normalization Guidance

- Report the exact paper text first when possible: `Google DeepMind`, not just `Google`.
- If a department/lab is meaningful for recruiting, keep it: `Tencent Hunyuan`, `Alibaba Qwen`, `Meta Reality Labs`.
- If the paper lists parent and lab together, keep both in one label: `Alibaba DAMO Academy`.
- If two independent companies appear, list both separately.
- If an author has a company email but the paper affiliation is a university only, do not classify as industry unless another paper source confirms the company affiliation.
